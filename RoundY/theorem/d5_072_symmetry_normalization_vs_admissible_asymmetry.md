This note gives a stable descriptive home for the symmetry statement originally
recorded in:

## Statement

The chain offset is removable on the larger exact theorem-side state space, but
it is not removable in the currently checked admissible/local picture.

So:

- the theorem-side clock is globally normalizable;
- the admissible/local quotient still exhibits asymmetry across chains.

This is the clean conceptual split behind the later bridge/globalization notes.
